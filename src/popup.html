YouTube Keyboard Shortcuts

  • Control+I - Hide/show YouTube controls
  • t - Toggle theater mode"
  • Spacebar or k - Play/Pause
  • m - Mute/unmute the video
  • Left/Right arrow on the seek bar - Seek backward/forward 5 seconds
  • j - Seek backward 10 seconds in player
  • l - Seek forward 10 seconds in player
  • . - Skip to the next frame while paused
  • , - Go back to the previous frame while paused
  • > - Speed up the video playback rate
  • < - Slow down the video playback rate
  • Home and End - Home/End on the seek bar - Seek to the beginning/last seconds of the video
  • Up/Down arrow on the seek bar - Increase/Decrease volume 5%
  • Numbers 1 to 9 - Seek to the 10% to 90% of the video
  • Number 0 - Seek to the beginning of the video
  • f or double click - Activate full screen
  • c - Activate closed captions and subtitles
  • Shift+N - Move to the next video
  • Shift+P - Move to the previous video
  • i - Open the Miniplayer
